About Me

Reflecting on my career change from customer service to software engineering fills me with a sense of pride and accomplishment. I was working as an Operations Lead at Lyft when I saw how technology could revolutionize people's lives. This inspired me to dive headfirst into the world of software engineering, and I enrolled in a 12-week intensive program at General Assembly. Drawing on my skills in problem-solving, teamwork, and operations, I quickly gained the technical skills necessary to become a software engineer.

Now, I'm excited to use my newfound abilities to create software solutions that have a positive impact on people's lives and businesses. When I'm not coding, I love to indulge my passions for video games, traveling, and trying new foods in the Bay Area. My sense of adventure and excitement for exploring new places and cuisines is something that I bring to my work as a software engineer.

Overall, I'm immensely proud of my career change and the journey that led me here. It hasn't been without its challenges, but the rewards have been well worth it. I'm eager to continue growing in my role as a software engineer and making a positive impact in the world of technology.
